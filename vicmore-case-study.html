Vicmore

Turning a trusted home health agency with no online presence into a discoverable, trustworthy digital destination.

OverView

Vicmore, a home health agency, had no website at all. In an era where families search for care online, this absence meant potential clients couldn't find them, understand their services, or even reach out. Yet Vicmore continued to retain clients through word of mouth alone, proving their care was exceptional. As a result, I designed, coded, and deployed a website to bridge the gap

Problem

Vicmore, a home health agency, currently lacks a digital presence, operating without a website in an era where consumers primarily discover and evaluate services online. This absence creates a significant barrier for potential clients, who have no clear way to understand the company's offerings, assess its credibility, or initiate contact. Despite this challenge, Vicmore demonstrably attains and retains clients, indicating that its services are both essential and delivered to a high standard.

Solution

This project aims to bridge that gap by designing and developing a comprehensive website. The primary objectives are to clearly communicate Vicmore's services, streamline the contact process, and establish a strong local SEO foundation.

Discovery

Research

To establish a foundation for the Vicmore redesign, I analyzed industry best practices for successful healthcare websites. I studied how top agencies build trust by demystifying complex information, learning that users navigating home care for the first time are easily overwhelmed by jargon and opaque processes. From this research, I identified key principles to guide my design: prioritizing clarity and empathy, explaining services in plain language, and using authentic visuals to tell a compassionate story. I also investigated how leading competitors address cost and credibility anxieties, discovering the importance of transparent insurance and payment details, caregiver profiles to humanize the service, and patient testimonials for social proof. Utilizing these insights, I developed a strategic content framework and SEO approach—focusing on descriptive, long-tail keywords—to ensure Vicmore connects with families at the precise moment they are seeking solutions.

Affinity Maps

After sorting notes from various sources, I initiated the creation of affinity maps, organizing similar information, feelings, and thoughts into cohesive groups. This approach allowed for the storage of information in a concise and actionable manner, facilitating effective analysis and decision-making processes.

Persona

The synthesis of secondary research revealed clear patterns in user challenges and goals. To personify these insights and ensure a user-centered approach, I developed a detailed persona. This persona acts as a vital reference point, grounding all subsequent design decisions in a concrete understanding of our target user.

Ideation

Problem Statements (HMW)

Drawing from my previous research, I crafted "How Might We" statements aimed at addressing the common challenges identified, serving as key reference points for feature creation and functionality determination. These statements provided actionable insights into potential solutions and guided the development process towards addressing user needs effectively.

  • How might we convey to users they are in the right place
  • How can we make reaching out to inquire about services be simple and fast?
  • How might we make financial information transparent and easy to understand?

    • Site Map

    Guided by user research, I created a streamlined sitemap to form the structural foundation of the redesign. This process focused on organizing key pages and their content to ensure logical flow and intuitive navigation. This results in a blueprint that seamlessly guides users to their goals, directly enhancing usability and overall satisfaction.

    Sketching

    With the user journey defined, I translated task flows into initial sketches. This phase was crucial for rapidly iterating on the core layout and functionality, ensuring a foundation that balanced simplicity with a comprehensive user experience before committing to higher-fidelity designs.

    Wire Frames

    Following the initial sketching phase, it was time to transition to a more functional and pragmatic medium. I began to create wireframes, which served as the foundational framework for the user interface. These wireframes laid the groundwork, delineating the essential structure and functionality of the interface.

    Design

    Style Guide

    Developing a style guide was paramount for maintaining the brand's personality and ensuring design consistency. It provided structure and guidance, ensuring uniformity across elements such as color, typography, buttons, and icons. This ensured a cohesive and harmonious visual identity throughout the brand's digital presence.

    Devlopment

    I built the site using React for its component-based architecture, making the code reusable and easy to scale. For styling, I used Tailwind CSS, which allowed me to rapidly implement a responsive, accessible design while maintaining complete control over the visual details. The result is a lightweight, fast-loading website that performs beautifully on any device.
