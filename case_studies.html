Texas Southern University

At Texas Southern University, I served as the UX Intern on the Marketing and Communications team. During my time there, I contributed to improving the site's user experience by fixing broken links, updating UI components on contact pages, and refining navigation elements. I also played a key role in the university's CMS migration and helped develop conceptual app designs presented in team meetings for a proposed TSU mobile experience.

FBref Redesign

As an avid user of FBref, I appreciate its depth of information, but navigating the website can be challenging due to the sheer volume of data. Finding specific information can be difficult and at times daunting, this prompted me to undertake the research and redesign efforts to enhance the user experience.

Double Barrel Redesign

For the Double Barrel redesign, I took on the challenge of enhancing an e-commerce experience. My goal was to keep users focused on the brand's purpose, encouraging them to inquire about their own custom smoker while incorporating visual interest to create a more engaging and memorable interaction.
