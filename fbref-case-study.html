FBref Redesign

Enhancing the user experience for football statistics

OverView

As an avid user of FBref, I appreciate its depth of information, but navigating the website can be challenging due to the sheer volume of data. Finding specific information can be difficult and at times daunting, this prompted me to undertake the research and redesign efforts to enhance the user experience.

Problem

Users frequently encounter frustration and difficulty interpreting statistics on FBref, compounded by challenges in reading and locating information due to congested design, resulting in information overload.

Solution

Past solutions, such as glossary models, have been instrumental in mitigating the unknown, while the introduction of readable and manageable data, facilitated through thoughtful design, enhances user experience.

Discovery

Research

Leveraging FBref's worldwide presence, I extensively searched various social media platforms to gather and directly engage with users for valuable insights. Additionally, feedback obtained from the Sports Reference group helped inform my design decisions. Utilizing the data accumulated, I developed personas and affinity maps to gain a deeper understanding of the diverse user base.

Affinity Maps

After sorting notes from various sources, I initiated the creation of affinity maps, organizing similar information, feelings, and thoughts into cohesive groups. This approach allowed for the storage of information in a concise and actionable manner, facilitating effective analysis and decision-making processes.

Persona

I utilized the affinity map to develop a persona, which personalized the audience and played a crucial role in shaping my design decisions. By centering the development process around the persona, users remained at the core, ensuring that their needs and preferences were prioritized throughout the design process.

Ideation

Problem Statements (HMW)

Drawing from my previous research, I crafted "How Might We" statements aimed at addressing the common challenges identified, serving as key reference points for feature creation and functionality determination. These statements provided actionable insights into potential solutions and guided the development process towards addressing user needs effectively.

  • HMW present data in a way that is visually palatable
  • HMW integrate different more informative graphs
  • HMW improve grouping of information to improve searching
  • HMW use color to alleviate stress and fatigue
  • HMW use simplicity to give users more

    • Site Map

    With a clear understanding of user objectives, I initiated the creation of a sitemap. This involved outlining the key pages of the application and determining the content for each page. Prioritizing organization, I structured the sitemap to ensure continuity and facilitate seamless navigation for users.

    Sketching

    With a clear understanding of the user journey, I commenced the sketching phase, translating ideas onto paper. I mapped out the screens users would encounter, ensuring simplicity and thoroughness in the sketches. This process allowed for visualizing the app's layout and interactions, laying a solid foundation for further development.

    Wire Frames

    Following the sketching phase, I transitioned to creating low-fidelity wireframes to translate the initial concepts into a functional yet reasonable medium. These wireframes formed the underlying structure of the user interface, providing a clear blueprint for further development.

    Design

    Style Guide

    Developing a style guide was paramount for maintaining the brand's personality and ensuring design consistency. It provided structure and guidance, ensuring uniformity across elements such as color, typography, buttons, and icons. This ensured a cohesive and harmonious visual identity throughout the brand's digital presence.

    Prototype

    I utilized Figma to create a prototype of my application, bringing its design to life for usability evaluation. The prototype enabled me to navigate through different sections, identifying and addressing any issues along the way. Experiencing the design as if it were a real application provided valuable insights into user interactions. This hands-on approach enhanced my understanding of functionality and guided improvements to enhance the overall user experience.

    Reflection

    This project has been instrumental in my growth as a designer, offering invaluable learning opportunities. I refined my skills in utilizing layout grids, color styles, and text styles to ensure a polished user interface. Moreover, I acquired new skills in maintaining consistency across frames using components and variables, and mastered frames and autolayout techniques for cohesive and responsive designs. Working on an already launched product posed a refreshing challenge, prompting me to adapt my approach accordingly. I am immensely grateful for the support and guidance provided by A dam Darowski from Sports Reference, whose valuable insights greatly contributed to the redesign of FBref.
